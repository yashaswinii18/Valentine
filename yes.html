Happy Valentine’s Day to my Valentine, forever. ❤️

I know I overthink sometimes, and I know the distance makes it worse. When you don’t reply, my mind runs everywhere — not because I doubt you, but because I care too much.

I don’t need big things from you; I just need to know you’re okay, you reached home safe, and you’re thinking of me sometimes.

Being away from you isn’t easy, but loving you makes it worth it. Even on the days I miss you a little extra, you’re still my safe place.

Always you.
